## Overview

This section contains the standard operating procedures used across the FAT Racing programme. SOPs are the single source of truth for how we prepare, operate, and review — follow them to ensure consistency across every event and every team member.

<Note>
  Always check you're reading the latest version of an SOP before a race weekend. Procedures are updated after each event based on lessons learned.
</Note>
