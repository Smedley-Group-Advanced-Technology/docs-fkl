## Overview

Use this checklist to stay on top of everything needed to deliver a successful race weekend. Work through each section in order and check off items as you go.

<Note>
  Complete all pre-event items at least 24 hours before the event starts to avoid last-minute issues.
</Note>

---

## Pre-Event

Get everything in place before the event begins.

### Setup & Logistics

- [ ] Confirm date, time, and venue (or platform for virtual events)
- [ ] Send invitations and track RSVPs
- [ ] Finalise the agenda and share it with all participants
- [ ] Brief speakers, hosts, or facilitators on their roles
- [ ] Prepare and test any equipment, tools, or software you'll be using

### Communications

- [ ] Send a reminder to attendees 48 hours before the event
- [ ] Share joining instructions or directions 24 hours before
- [ ] Confirm attendance with key speakers or stakeholders

### Content & Materials

- [ ] Prepare presentations, handouts, or supporting materials
- [ ] Review and rehearse any demos or live walkthroughs
- [ ] Set up registration, check-in, or access links

<Warning>
  Double-check that all shared links, logins, and access credentials are working before the event day.
</Warning>
