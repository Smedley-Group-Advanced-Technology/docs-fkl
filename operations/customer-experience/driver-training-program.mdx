## Overview

The Driver Training Program (R2R) requires up-to-date driver data to be exported from the OD after sign-on and re-imported into the Driver Training software. This ensures kart allocation and coach tablet assignments are correct for each session.

---

## Exporting Driver Data for R2R

<Note>
  There are occasions where drivers appear on the OD due to purchasing season passes but may not be attending a specific session. When exporting, ensure the driver names match those on the Sign On site. Drivers can be ticked or unticked from the list on export.
</Note>

Before exporting, update the OD with any changes that apply — weight, height, class, or power level — then export the updated data to re-import into the Driver Training software.

---

## Power Mode to Class Mappings

When exporting driver data, the class assigned must match the driver's power mode exactly. Use the mappings below:

| Power Mode | Class |
|--|--|
| 1, 2, or 3 | Bambino R2R, Cadet R2R, or Junior R2R |
| CHAMP | Bambino, Cadet, Cadet Light, Junior, or Junior Light |

<Note>
  If a driver has completed the Driver Training Program and has booked a Season Pass or Individual Race Ticket, their power level should be set to the relevant championship — **REG** for a Regional Championship ticket or **PRO** for a Pro Championship ticket.
</Note>
