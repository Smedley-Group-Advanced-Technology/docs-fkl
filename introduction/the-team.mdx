## Overview

FAT Karting League operates across multiple hubs globally, with a central team overseeing the programme and hub-level teams running day-to-day operations at each location.

This page gives an overview of the key roles and contacts across the organisation. For a full breakdown of what each role is responsible for, see the [Roles & Responsibilities](/operations/roles/index) section.

---

## Organisation Structure

FKL is structured across three layers:

- **Central** — programme leadership, engineering, software, and remote support operating across all hubs
- **Hub Management** — hub-level leadership responsible for operations at a specific location
- **Event Staff** — role-based staff deployed at events, including Charging Technicians, Customer Experience Leads, and contractors
