## Welcome

Welcome to the FAT Karting League internal playbook — the single source of truth for how we operate, build, and grow the FKL programme globally.

Whether you're joining us as a full-time team member, a contractor, or a race weekend employee, this playbook is designed to give you everything you need to do your job well. From day-to-day SOPs to role-specific guides, technical documentation to company processes — it's all here.
